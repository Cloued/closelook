Reviews

Closelook will provide the readers with trustworthy and relavant reviews to support reader in understanding whether the product/entertainment is suitable for them

Focus

Focus will be designed to becoome a page where topics are heavily investigated to be easily understood by readers. Closelook will do the heavy lifting while providing a simple and easy to understand platform for the readers to enjoy.

Analytics page will aim to provide a stream of analytical news on interesting and relevant topics to provie a stronger understanding of the topics itself.
